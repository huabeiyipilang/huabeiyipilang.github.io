情况是这样的~：

1、用的StoryBoard

2、跳转代码：

[objc]
DetailViewController* detailController = [[DetailViewController alloc]init];
[self presentViewController:detailController animated:YES completion:nil];
[/objc]

解决：
1、在StoryBoard中，给DetailViewController的Storyboard ID命名为detail_controller
2、获取DetailViewController实例的代码改为
[objc]
DetailViewController* detailController = [self.storyboard instantiateViewControllerWithIdentifier:@"detail_controller"];
[/objc]
